EVENTS

BioTalks

The real talk !

At BioTalks we examine trends in technology, how they affect the way we live and do business, and the problems they solve. We discuss methodologies pertaining to the Computational Biology domain.

On special occasions, we will invite industry veterans to come and talk about the problems they are working on and their experience so far.

Transcribe O'Bytes

Transcribe ideas into reality!

A hackathon with a twist! Where we fabricate ideas, it is where innovative solutions will be crafted for worldwide healthcare problems.

According to the format of the hackathon we will give a theme. We will pose a healthcare/biological problem and the participants ought to come up with innovative solutions to the problem.

BioCode

Algorithmic thinking!

Algorithms to computational biology is like bread to butter. Our goal at BCode will be to make the students familiar to CB just like DS and Algorithms.

Seniors and some time professors will take these sessions. The format will be like the tutorial sessions we have for our compulsory courses, an illustration followed by hands on session.

NEWSLETTER

A fortnightly newsletter

We will publish our newsletter 'BioBits' fortnightly. We will send it to students@iiitd.ac.in in digital format and will put up a printed version on notice boards. The content will largely pertain to the advancements in computational biology domain and important things happening in the CCB dept. of our college. We will attach resources for further reading and in some issues we might also include link to a problem (Rosalind's bioinformatics stronghold is a good resource). We shall consult with the professors and seniors for topics to include in every issue.
